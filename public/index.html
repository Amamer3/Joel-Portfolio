About me

I am a Data analyst and Shiny developer with a strong foundation in building dynamic and user-friendly web applications. With expertise in R, Shiny, and a range of complementary tools like Python, SQL, HTML, Javascript and CSS, I specialize in transforming complex data into interactive dashboards and solutions that drive actionable insights. My work includes designing high-performance Shiny applications for air quality monitoring, incorporating user authentication, and creating customized visualizations with ggplot and leaflet.

My academic background, which includes a Master’s degrees in Quantitative Research Methodology and Comparative Social Research, provides a solid framework for leveraging data to address real-world challenges. Currently, I work as an air quality analyst, where I combine my technical skills with sociological insights to build scalable applications for sensor calibration and environmental monitoring. Whether it’s developing dashboards, optimizing app performance through parallel programming, or integrating APIs seamlessly, I am passionate about delivering robust and impactful solutions.

If you’re looking for a developer who combines technical expertise, creativity, and a focus on user experience, I’d love to collaborate on your next project!
